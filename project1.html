5 Step Guide to Boost Your Basic Hygiene Practices at "Home" or at "Work"/"In Public"

1.Wash Hands Frequently

2.Don't Touch Your Face!

3.Keep Your Distance (1m Distance)

4.Don't Shake Hands or Hug People

5.Sanitise Frequently Used Surfaces/"High-Touch Surfaces" (Door Handles, Cellphones, Light Switches, Remote Controls etc.)

Bonus Tips:

6.Don't Cough or Sneeze Into Your Hands!

7.Wear A Facemask In Public to Reduce Chances of Contracting Covid-19 Through The Air

8.Be Courteous/Care About Other's - If You Are Sick Then Stay At Home!

9.Read News About The Covid-19 Virus - Only Read "Trusted Sources" - Watch Out for "Fake News"!
